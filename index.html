Pet Life - The Story of Seth and Scott

This is a documentation of the life of my two pets called Seth and Scott. They are both seven months old male German Shepherd and American Eskimo breed. Having pets takes a lot of commitment and responsibility, we mustn't leave out the finances that goes into breeding healthy dogs..

Health Benefits of Having Pets

There are many health benefits of having pets,however,below are two you must know.

  1. Keeping pets helps reduce blood pressure.
  2. It has been proven that having pets helps to fight depression and other mental health issues.
